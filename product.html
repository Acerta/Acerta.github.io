Our Product

Anomalies can go unnoticed in your vehicles, causing many problems in the future. Detecting an anomaly late causes massive product recalls, long equipment downtimes and ultimately huge costs to your organization. Acerta allows to quickly diagnose anomalies to discover critical problems in your vehicles through our dynamic machine learning platform. This leads to faster diagnostic times and increased manufacturing uptime.

Key Features

Root Cause Analysis

Finding anomalies isn’t very useful if you can’t figure out what caused it. We establish the root cause of the anomaly by identifying trends leading to the problem and the signals responsible.
