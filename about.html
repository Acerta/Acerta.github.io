About Us

We are a team of engineers and data scientists who started Acerta with the goal of using data to automatically detect defects and malfunctions in modern machinery. These machines are increasingly becoming more connected and autonomous, making remote diagnostics and automated failure detection and prediction.

Our founders Greta and Sebastian were also closely involved with automotive systems. Greta was working on advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) at Magna during that time. Sebastian has been the associate director of WatCar, Waterloo’s autonomous vehicle program. Due to their involvement in the automotive area they saw the growing need for better diagnostic solutions, and thus this technology.

Vehicles are generating massive amounts of data that is being collected for the first time both during manufacturing and on the road. This data is a huge and largely untapped source that can be used to detect a variety of problems in the vehicle.
